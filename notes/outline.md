# Outline

The emerging order and purpose of the essay's sections. Only evidence-cleared material is
ordered here; deferred research remains in `ideas.md` and `research/`.

## Baseline essay

1. **The fair trade** - equal starts, uniform encounters, the poorer-wealth stake, and the
   precise zero-expected-drift meaning of fairness.
2. **Watch several worlds** - seeded runs show temporary reversals and different winners
   rather than presenting one trajectory as proof.
3. **Measure the room** - introduce top share and the finite-population Gini while the
   distribution concentrates.
4. **The limiting result** - state almost-sure condensation for finite `N` and
   `0 < beta < 1`, with the asymptotic qualifier intact.
5. **What the toy leaves out** - production, income, debt, work, bargaining, institutions,
   policy, and claims about actual people.

## Gated later sections

- Additive exchange contrast after the exponential-fit replication.
- Static topology after confirmatory local-rich and degree-scaling results.
- Retrospective-story and levy interactions as explicitly labeled widgets, not evidence
  for psychology or real policy.
