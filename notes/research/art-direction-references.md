## Admission checklist

- Verify the creator and canonical source.
- Record the license or mark it "reference only; no asset reuse."
- Name the narrow property being studied: line economy, palette, spacing, motion, or
  information hierarchy.
- Reject references whose identity cues encourage stereotypes or bind appearance to
  economic worth.
- Test the derived design at crowded simulation scale, reduced motion, grayscale, and
  both LTR and future RTL direction.
